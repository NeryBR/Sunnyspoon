About Sunny Spoon

A modern family restaurant serving fresh, seasonal dishes with love.

Our Story

Sunny Spoon started in 2020 as a small kitchen project. Our founder wanted to combine traditional Kazakh hospitality with modern culinary techniques.

We bake bread daily, source from local farms, and adapt menus to the season. Our goal is to create food that feels like home.

Our Values

  • Fresh ingredients
  • Sustainable sourcing
  • Welcoming atmosphere
  • Community events and workshops
